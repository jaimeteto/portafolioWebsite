Bodyshop Inventory APP

❮ ❯

The Inventory Parts App was developed to streamline the management of vehicle parts at the body shop where I worked. Built using Java and Android Studio, the app integrates two key libraries: iText PDF Reader for extracting data from PDF estimates and ZXing Barcode Reader for scanning and cataloging parts by barcode. These features allowed for automated entry of parts data, reducing manual errors and improving efficiency in tracking inventory. By automating the process of data extraction and barcode reading, the app helped the shop better manage its inventory, minimize downtime, and ensure that the right parts were always available for repairs.
