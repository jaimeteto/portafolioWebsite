Web Developer Intern

Visit Organization

Overview

At Rise Up Roses, I worked on improving the nonprofit’s website and digital presence. My role included both design and development work, helping create a better experience for visitors while supporting the organization’s mission through a clearer and more effective website.

Tech Stack

WordPress, Figma, HTML, CSS, plugin configuration, website content management

Key Contributions

  • Updated and improved website pages to make them more user-friendly
  • Created wireframes and design ideas in Figma before implementation
  • Worked on adding new features and improving layout consistency
  • Helped align the site structure with the organization’s goals and audience
  • Contributed to making the site feel more modern, organized, and easier to navigate

Challenges & Problem Solving

One challenge was balancing design improvements with the practical limits of the site’s existing setup. I had to think about usability, responsiveness, and content clarity while working within WordPress tools and plugin-based workflows. This helped me better understand how to make design decisions that are both realistic and impactful.
