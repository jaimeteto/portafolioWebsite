Conect Four APP

❮ ❯

This project implements a Connect 4 AI using the Minimax algorithm with Alpha-Beta Pruning, based on code originally provided by a developer on YouTube. However, several modifications and new features have been added to enhance the gameplay and make the AI more challenging. One of the major changes is the winning condition. In the original game, a player wins by connecting four discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). In this modified version, a player wins by filling a 2x2 square with their discs, adding a new strategic element to the game. Additionally, new features were introduced to improve the AI's intelligence. The AI difficulty levels were enhanced by implementing a probability-based decision-making system at lower levels. For example, at lower difficulties, there is a chance the AI will make random moves instead of relying purely on the Minimax algorithm, making it less predictable. As the difficulty increases, this randomness decreases, allowing the AI to make more optimal decisions by fully utilizing the Alpha-Beta Pruning feature.
